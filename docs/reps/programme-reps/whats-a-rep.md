# What's a Rep?

Programme Reps make vital changes to your programme to ensure a positive student experience. They work with both staff and students to ensure all students get the most amazing academic experience while at uni.

As a rep you will have the chance to gain greater understanding of how your degree works and develop the leadership skills that employers have told us they are looking for! This will involve working collaboratively with key members of university staff and gaining insight into how the university is run at different levels.

Interested in standing to represent your peers, your course and yourself? Speak to your Programme Leader today or email [su.reps@northumbria.ac.uk](mailto:su.reps@northumbria.ac.uk).

You can find your Programme Rep’s details on your [Student Portal](https://myportal.northumbria.ac.uk/) under the ‘My Reps’ section and in your [Student Dashboard](https://student-dashboard.sums.su/my-reps) on our website.
