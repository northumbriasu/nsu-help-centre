# How do I work with other reps?

Working with other reps, like your School and/or Equality Reps, is a vital part of your role, as it enables you to tackle the bigger issues which don’t just affect your course.

As a Programme Rep you will be able to join the Whatsapp group chat set up by your School Rep. You can find a full list of links [here](https://mynsu-reps.kb.help/department-group-chats/).

You can also find all of your School and Equality Reps on the Student Voice page of our website [here](https://mynsu.co.uk/student-voice). Simply click on the relevant tile and you will be able to send an email directly to the rep.

If you aren't sure who you want to reach out to, we would recommend that you use Coursemate, the feedback platform on our website which can be accessed by logging into mynsu.co.uk with your uni credentials. Any submissions to Coursemate will be received by the Student Voice Team, who can assign these issues (as well as any positive feedback you'd like to share) to a relevant Leadership Rep or Sabbatical Officer. You can find more information about Coursemate [here](https://mynsu-reps.kb.help/coursemate/).
