# How can NSU support me with equity and inclusion?

### Workshops

We regularly run workshops on **Equity, Diversity, and Inclusion**to help students become better allies for marginalised groups.

We also run **Active Bystander** training throughout the year that covers how students can be active in helping and supporting others when faced with unacceptable behaviours, such as hate incidents, discrimination, sexual harassment, and violence.

All our workshops will be advertised on our [events calendar](https://mynsu.co.uk/events). Got a question or want to get involved? Email us at [su.reps@northumbria.ac.uk](mailto:su.reps@northumbria.ac.uk)

### **Anonymous tool for reporting unacceptable behaviours**

The Unacceptable Behaviours Policy and online tool is available for any student to seek support and/or report their concerns if you experience, or witness, unacceptable behaviour. You can also report anonymously.

The unacceptable behaviours policy does not only cover sexual misconduct, but also includes behaviour that undermines, humiliates, threatens, or hurts. This includes **bullying, discrimination, domestic violence, harassment, hate incidents, misogyny, abuse and violence, victimisation** or **violence**of any nature. Report [here](https://northumbria.service-now.com/ub).

### **Accessibility and Inclusion Team**

This team is dedicated to helping students from underrepresented groups and Widening Participation Backgrounds who may require additional support. They offer 1-1 support including academic, financial and wellbeing advice, support and guidance.

**Enquire via [Student Portal](https://myportal.northumbria.ac.uk/Help-and-support/Support-and-Wellbeing/Welfare-Support/Accessing-support-from-NU-Belong).**
