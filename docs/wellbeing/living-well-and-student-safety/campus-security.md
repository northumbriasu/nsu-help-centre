# Campus Security

Keeping you safe on campus is a responsibility we take very seriously. All students have the right to be able to come to Northumbria and study without the fear of harassment or unacceptable behaviour.   
   
Northumbria University has a dedicated security team who work around the clock 24/7 365 days a year to ensure that the campus is safe and secure.

**To contact security:**

*Emergency Number:* 3200 (0191 227 3200) Please note, this number is for emergency use only.

*General Enquiries:* 3999 (0191 227 3999)

Email: [CRSecurityTeams2@northumbria.ac.uk](mailto:CRSecurityTeams2@northumbria.ac.uk)

**Some of their duties are:**

- Protecting Staff, Students and Visitors​

- Protecting Research Facilities, Teaching Facilities and Accommodation

- Supporting pastoral care for students and staff

- Engaging with the wider community

- Promoting Crime awareness and preventing crime

**How do they achieve these aims:**

- Responding to all alerts on the Safezone app which is for all students to use.

- Providing a 24 hour, 365 days a year staffed control room.

- The monitoring of and response to Fire alarms, building alarms, Intruder Alarms, Lift Alarms IT Server Room Alarms.

- A one stop out of hours call point for dealing with all emergencies.

- Provision of Uniformed and Plain Clothes Security Officers.

Students can get Panic Alarms, Bike Locks and Anti spiking
