# Wellbeing Products 

At Northumbria Students' Union we fight for your right to access products which benefit your wellbeing such as free period products and condoms.   
  
We have wellbeing cubboards in the SU, Coach Lane Campus west and London Campus where you will find that we provide free condoms, pregnancy tests and STI testing kits all year round. This is because we want all students to be safe and protected while at university. They are free to access, all we ask is you only take what you need.   
  
We provide free period products in the bathrooms across campus, you will find them in the male, female and gender neutral bathrooms, we are a trans inclusive campus. We provide these to end period poverty on campus because noone should feel that they cannot attend because they do not have access to the products they need.   
  
We also provide free eco friendly period products such as period cups and reusable pad sets.   
  
These can be ordered here: <https://mynsu.co.uk/survey/Wellbeing-Product-Order>
