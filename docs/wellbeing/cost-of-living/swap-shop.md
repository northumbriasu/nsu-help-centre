# Swap Shop

![](https://cdn.livechat-files.com/api/file/kb/file/032d3277-4a39-4677-81f1-4a8ce74753d3/7658bfe0e8-03f3849f5fe6520af992.png)  
The Swap Shop is a sustainable eco-friendly way for all students to come and update their wardrobe without costing a penny.   
  
This is a safe space for LGBTQIA+ students and welcomes everyone.   
  
We are lucky to receive donations from the Pretty Little Things sample sale and so some of the items will be brand new.   
  
**How it works:**

- Simply clear out your wardrobe and drawers of any clothing you no longer want or wear and bring them into the Students' Union on the day of a Swap Shop.
- Hand them to the staff at the swap shop event and then you can browse clothing that has already been donated and take home any items you like.
- Any clothes which have been put out a number of times and not taken will be donated to a local charity.

**Rules:**

- For every 1 item you bring, you are able to take 2 items.
- If you do not bring any items then you are limited to 2 items.
- Please ensure all clothes that are donated have been washed and are in good condition.
- For health and safety reasons we are not able to accept underwear or swim wear.

*Please note:* *As all items are donations we are unable to ensure that all sizes are catered for at all events; however we regularly request donations for male/masculine sizes and plus/curvy sizes.*
