# Warm Space 

![](https://cdn.livechat-files.com/api/file/kb/file/032d3277-4a39-4677-81f1-4a8ce74753d3/f711aa7171-678c2f46742b154693ef.png)  
  
  
During Winter every Wednesday at Northumbria Students' Union we will be hosting a Warm Space.

This is so that everyone can come and keep themselves warm in our building with a free hot drink and some mindful activities such as arts and crafts, games and more.   
  
This is done in order to support students cost of living in the hopes of reducing costs of heating their home and allowing an open space for people to connect and and socialise.   
  
This initiative takes place in Escape at the Students union from 11am to 2pm every Wednesday.
