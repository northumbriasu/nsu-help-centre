# Sexual Health Services 

**Sexual Health Services**

- **Ambrose King Sexual Health Centre**

- **Address:** The Royal London Hospital, Mount Terrace, London E1 2BB
- **Services:** Offers comprehensive free and confidential NHS services including STI testing and treatment, rapid HIV testing, emergency contraception (pills and coils), general contraception, PrEP/PEP, and support/counselling.
- **Access:** Appointments are recommended and can be booked online. Walk-ins are generally for specific urgent cases like emergency contraception or PEP after HIV exposure.
- **Hours (Today, Thursday, Jan 15):** 9:10 AM–7:30 PM

- **TH CASH (Tower Hamlets Contraception and Sexual Health)**

- **Address:** Mile End Hospital, Bancroft Road, E1 4DG
- **Contact:** Call 020 7377 7870 to access services.

- **80 Leadenhall Street (Homerton Sexual Health)**

- **Address:** 80 Leadenhall St, London EC3A 3DH
- **Services:** Offers free sexual health services, including testing and contraception.
- **Access:** This is an **appointment-only** clinic. Appointments can be made by phone or online.
- **Hours (Today, Thursday, Jan 15):** 9 AM–4 PM

**Other Options**

- **Online Services:** For people without symptoms, the free online service **shl.uk** (Sexual Health London) provides home testing kits delivered by post.
- **GPs and Pharmacies:** Some GP surgeries and pharmacies also offer certain sexual health services, such as free emergency contraception or chlamydia screening kits.
- **National Sexual Health Helpline:** For general advice or information on young people's services, you can call the national helpline on 0300 123 7123.
