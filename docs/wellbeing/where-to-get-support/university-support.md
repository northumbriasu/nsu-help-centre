# University Support

# Accessing specialist support to stay on track with your studies

### One-to-one support is available to you if you experience issues either inside or outside of University that have an impact on your studies

We recognise that at certain times on your journey with us you may experience issues either inside or outside of University that have an impact on your studies. We offer a personalised and professional service to ensure that you have the support and guidance you need to get back on track.

Please find further information on the types of support you can access on your student portal
