# Out of Hours Emergency Support

**Samaritans**  
  
To talk about anything that is upsetting you, you can contact Samaritans 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. You can call 116 123 (free from any phone), email [jo@samaritans.org](mailto:jo@samaritans.org)

**Contact Campus Security or emergency services**

- Emergency Number: 3200 (0191 227 3200) **Please note, this number is for emergency use only.**
- General Enquiries: 3999 (0191 227 3999)
- Email: [CRSecurityTeams2@northumbria.ac.uk](mailto:CRSecurityTeams2@northumbria.ac.uk)

**Papyrus**  
  
If you are having thoughts of suicide or are concerned for a young person who might be you can contact HOPELINE247 for confidential support and practical advice.

- Call: [0800 068 4141](tel:08000684141)
- Text: [88247](sms:88247)
- Email: [pat@papyrus-uk.org](mailto:pat@papyrus-uk.org)

**SANEline**  
  
If you're experiencing a mental health problem or supporting someone else, you can call SANEline on 0300 304 7000 (4.30pm–10.30pm every day)  
**National Suicide Prevention Helpline UK**  
  
Offers a supportive listening service to anyone with thoughts of suicide. You can call the [National Suicide Prevention Helpline UK](https://www.spuk.org.uk/national-suicide-prevention-helpline-uk/ "National Suicide Prevention Helpline") on [0800 689 5652](tel:+44-08006895652) (6pm to midnight every day)**Shout**  
  
If you would prefer not to talk but want some mental health support, you could text SHOUT to [85258](sms:+44-85258). [Shout](https://giveusashout.org/ "Shout website") offers a confidential 24/7 text service providing support if you are in crisis and need immediate help.
